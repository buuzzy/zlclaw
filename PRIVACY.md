# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

**Last Updated: April 29, 2026**

## English

Sage ("the App") is developed by YIYANG CAI. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

### Data Collection

Sage does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers controlled by us. Specifically:

- **No account required**: The App can be used without creating an account.
- **No analytics or tracking**: We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
- **No personal data sold**: We do not sell, trade, or share personal information with third parties.

### Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- Chat history and session data
- User preferences and settings
- Cached financial data for display purposes

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

### Third-Party Services

The App connects to third-party financial data APIs to retrieve real-time market information (stock quotes, indices, financial reports, etc.). These requests contain only the query parameters (e.g., stock symbols) and do not include any personally identifiable information.

### Optional Account Features

If you choose to sign in (via email or OAuth), your authentication is handled by Supabase. Only your email address is stored for account identification purposes. You can delete your account at any time.

### Data Deletion

All locally stored data can be cleared by removing the App from your Mac. If you have an account, you may request data deletion by contacting us.

### Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact:

- GitHub: [https://github.com/buuzzy/zlclaw](https://github.com/buuzzy/zlclaw)
- Email: Open an issue on the GitHub repository

## 中文

Sage（以下简称"本应用"）由 YIYANG CAI 开发。本隐私政策说明本应用如何处理您的信息。

### 数据收集

本应用**不会**收集、存储或向我们控制的外部服务器传输任何个人数据。具体而言：

- **无需注册账号**：本应用无需创建账号即可使用
- **无分析或追踪**：我们不使用任何第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK
- **不出售个人数据**：我们不会向第三方出售、交易或分享个人信息

### 本地存储的数据

本应用仅在**您的设备本地**存储以下数据：

- 聊天历史和会话数据
- 用户偏好和设置
- 用于展示的金融数据缓存

这些数据保留在您的设备上，不会传输到我们的服务器。

### 第三方服务

本应用连接第三方金融数据 API 获取实时市场信息（股票报价、指数、财务报告等）。这些请求仅包含查询参数（如股票代码），不包含任何个人身份信息。

### 可选的账号功能

如果您选择登录（通过邮箱或 OAuth），身份验证由 Supabase 处理。仅存储您的邮箱地址用于账号识别。您可以随时删除账号。

### 数据删除

您可以通过从 Mac 上删除本应用来清除所有本地存储的数据。如果您拥有账号，可通过联系我们申请数据删除。

### 联系方式

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过以下方式联系：

- GitHub: [https://github.com/buuzzy/zlclaw](https://github.com/buuzzy/zlclaw)
